Welcome to this hub of information about the 2020 election! There are so many candidates running in 2020 that it can be very difficult to keep up. Our mission is to share the most important information about the most important candidates in this election. On this page you'll find information about numerous candidates, tables showing current polling and fundraising numbers, information about us, and even a bibliography of our sources!
